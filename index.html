Taxi Sharing

Fast, convenient and cheap!

  • View Prototype

The Problem

Taxis are known for their convenience. They take you from door to door really fast, but price is often a problem. Sharing a taxi with someone cuts the price in half, but finding someone to share with is not always easy.

During rush hours, or in the middle of the night on weekends it's really hard to get a taxi and you have to stand in a queue for half an hour. At those times sharing a taxi would not only save you money, but also time!

The Solution

We decided to solve your problems using a device almost everybody has in their pocket: a smartphone. Using our application you will be able to find a mate to share a ride with.

The use of the application is really simple. It's a four step process we will walk you through.

Step 1: Route planning

The first step is to let us know where you want to go. You may enter an address or pick a place on the map.

If you are already sharing a taxi with someone, like a friend or two, you can let us know here.

We will search for people who plan to go on a similar route and will fit into a taxi with you.

Step 2: Matchmaking

As soon as we find someone we will let you know. You can view other people's location, rating.

We also estimate how long the ride will take and how much it will cost for you.

You can choose who to share a ride with by sending them a request. Once they respond positively, you have a new taxi sharing mate!

Step 3: Meet & greet

Meeting with your new taxi sharing mate isn't easy. We help you by providing each other's locations and a chat where you can discuss where to meet.

You can also order a taxi from the application to an address if you want to.

Once you found each other, just hop into the taxi and take the ride. You can always split the cost of the ride in the taxi, even if you want to pay by card.

Step 4: Rate experience

If you reached your goal, the application will ask you to rate your experience.

You can rate the taxi driver and the taxi sharing mates seperately. This will help other people find nice people to share a ride with.

Thanks for using the application and we hope to see you again soon!
